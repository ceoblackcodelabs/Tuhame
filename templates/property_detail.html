About This Property

A bright, spacious 3-bedroom apartment on the 7th floor of Kilimani Heights, one of Nairobi's most sought-after residential buildings. Floor-to-ceiling windows offer panoramic views of the city and the Nairobi National Park in the distance.

The open-plan living and dining area flows onto a generous balcony. The modern kitchen features granite worktops and integrated appliances. The master suite has an en-suite bathroom with a walk-in shower and a dressing room. Two further bedrooms share a well-appointed family bathroom.
